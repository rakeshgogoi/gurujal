# Real Nature in Restored Landscapes

An experiential journey on nature-based transformation for climate adaptation & resilience. 21 February 2026 BSF Bhondsi | Khandewla Well | Triveni Pond, Bhokarka About the Program

Hosted by GuruJal & Prana Earth under Delhi Climate Innovation Week 2026, this immersive site visit offers a rare opportunity to witness real, on-ground climate-resilient solutions across restored landscapes.

Move beyond dialogue and explore how nature-based solutions are transforming ecosystems, communities, and climate adaptation pathways in practice.

What You Will Experience ✔ Restored water bodies & functional recharge systems ✔ Revived traditional wells & heritage structures ✔ Biodiversity regeneration in action ✔ Community-led conservation models ✔ Climate adaptation strategies on ground Who Should Attend

Practitioners, policymakers, corporates, researchers, students, and sustainability leaders seeking practical, scalable models for climate resilience and water security.

## About this publication

The **Standard Operating Procedure (SoP) for Wells** captures GuruJal's
methodology for the assessment, rejuvenation, and ongoing care of traditional
open wells across India. It is intended as a practical handbook for
practitioners, community groups, and government officers undertaking similar
restoration work.

### What's inside

- Site assessment & baseline ecology
- Structural and hydrological evaluation
- Step-by-step restoration protocols
- Community engagement guidance
- Long-term monitoring framework

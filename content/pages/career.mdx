# Careers at GuruJal

### Work With Purpose

At GuruJal, we believe growth is mutual. We look for people ready to lead the sector and grow with a dedicated team of peers and mentors. Join us in driving evidence-based change through water conservation, community engagement, nature-based solutions, and technological innovation.

### Diversity & Inclusion

GuruJal welcomes applicants from all backgrounds. We are committed to an inclusive hiring process.

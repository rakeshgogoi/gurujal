## About this publication

**Wells of Gurugram** documents the city's network of historic open wells —
once the backbone of the region's water security — and assesses their
present condition, ownership patterns, and revival potential. The report
combines on-ground surveys with hydrological analysis to make the case for
treating wells as integral to Gurugram's water future.

### What's inside

- A census of identified wells across the district
- Condition assessment and classification
- Hydrological context (aquifer linkage, recharge zones)
- Community and institutional ownership map
- Recommendations for revival and stewardship

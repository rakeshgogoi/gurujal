## About this publication

The **Pond Rejuvenation SOP** is GuruJal's field-tested methodology for
restoring ponds — from the first baseline survey through structural works,
biodiversity revival, and the long-term governance arrangements that keep
revived ponds healthy. It is designed to be picked up by any community,
NGO, or local government wanting to do this work well.

### What's inside

- Pre-restoration baseline survey
- Bathymetric, hydrological, and ecological assessment
- Restoration plan templates
- Community stewardship models
- Post-restoration monitoring & evaluation
